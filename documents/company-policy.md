# Company Vacation Policy

All employees are entitled to 25 days of paid vacation per year. Vacation days must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal. Unused vacation days can be carried over to the next year, up to a maximum of 5 days. Employees who have been with the company for more than 5 years receive an additional 5 vacation days per year.

# Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. On remote days, employees must be available on Slack between 9am and 5pm. All team meetings must be attended via video call. A stable internet connection is required for remote work.

# Expense Policy

Business expenses over $50 require manager approval. Receipts must be submitted within 30 days. Travel expenses are reimbursed at the standard company rate. Meals during business travel are capped at $75 per day.
